# Cookie-Sales-Data-Dashboard
This dataset tracks cookie sales, profit, and unit volume across five countries (India, Malaysia, Philippines, UK, USA) for six product types. It contains 5 populated sheets:

Sheet1 – Raw transaction-level data: country, product, units sold, sales revenue, purchase cost, profit, date & month.

Sheet2 – Monthly units sold (Jan–Dec 2020).

Sheet3 – Monthly total profit (matches grand total in other sheets).

Sheet4 – Profit pivot by country & product.

Sheet5 – Total sales revenue by product.
